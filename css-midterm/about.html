About the Designer

 

Ellie is studying in the Environmental Design program at the University of Colorado Boulder. She is currently a junior but planning to graduate a year early with a specialization in Urban Planning. Ellie graduated high school with an Associate’s of Arts through concurrent enrollment and is also working towards a minor in Creative Technology and Design while in college. This past summer Ellie studied abroad in Medellín, Columbia as part of a study abroad program offered by her school.

Ellie likes to create in several forms and besides her projects done during school, she also enjoys oil pastel drawings, photography, and mixed media projects. In her free time, Ellie likes to read and write. While in school Ellie is president of the APAS club as well as a member of the CU Boulder Freeskiing team and the Women’s Ultimate B Team.
